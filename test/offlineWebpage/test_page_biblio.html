BiblioCaf

Surrounded by glass, view the vivid seasonal changes while enjoying your selection – indoors with ample booth, table and comfortable circle chairs or outdoors with patio seating. Features:

  • specialty coffees and teas
  • our signature five star sandwiches, wraps and buns
  • salads
  • pastry rolls
  • samosas
  • cakes, squares, pastries and other sweets

*Please note: BiblioCafé is closed statutory holidays.
